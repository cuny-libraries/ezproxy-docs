Help!

Access to the library's electronic resources is granted to active students, staff, and faculty members at {{ campus_name }}. Professors emeriti also have access to these resources.

Authentication is done through CUNY Login. This centralized login system ensures a safe and streamlined experience for all authorized users.

Problem?

If you encounter any issues with CUNY Login, please reach out to the IT help desk for assistance. When reporting the problem, be sure to provide essential details such as your full name, CUNY Login username, and EMPLID. Including this information will enable them to address and resolve your concerns more efficiently.

If you have questions about the library or need research assistance, ask a librarian.
